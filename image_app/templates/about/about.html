What is Steganography?

Steganography involves encoding messages into images so that they are hidden in plain sight. Our tool leverages the power of modern algorithms to safely encrypt and hide messages inside image files, making it nearly impossible for unauthorized individuals to detect or retrieve them.

Advanced LSB Algorithm

How Does It Work?

Our platform allows users to upload an image and securely encode their message into it. The hidden text remains invisible and can only be extracted by using the correct decryption key or process, ensuring that your messages stay secure and confidential.

The Process

Image Analysis

Our advanced algorithms analyze your cover image to identify the optimal pixels for data embedding, ensuring maximum capacity while maintaining visual integrity.

Message Encryption

Your secret message is encrypted using military-grade AES-256 encryption before being prepared for embedding, adding an extra layer of security to your data.

Invisible Embedding

The encrypted data is embedded into the least significant bits of the image pixels using advanced LSB steganography, making it completely undetectable to the human eye.
